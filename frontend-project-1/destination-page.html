hispaniola

caribbean paradise

hispaniona is the 22nd-largest island in the worls,located in the caribbean island group,the greater antilles,it us the second largest island in the caribbean after cuba,and the tenth most populours islandin the world

hispaniola is the site of the first permanent eutopean settiement in the americas,founded by christopher columbous on his voyages in 1492 and 1493

geography

hisponiola is the second largest island of the west indies,with an area of about 76,129 km/sq.the name given to the island was columbous. hispaniola means "small spain".the tainos called the island "Quisqueya",mother earth.

from a geological point of view,the island of hisponiola is part of two branches of the north america cordillera system and is about 660 km long,250km wide and extend west-easterly.in the west,the island od hisponiola is seperated bt the approximately 90km wide windward passage from cuba and in the east by the mona passage of puerto rico,which is up to 110km wide.

on the island od hisponiola lie five large mountains chains.the heighest mountains of the caribbean are in the dominican republic.the heighest mountain in the pico duarte with 3,098 meters above the sea level.also the deepest point of the caribbean lies on hisponiola:the salt lake enriquillo in the southwest of the dominican republic.

hisponiola offers four vegetation zones and five climatic zones,more than 5,000 plant species,many of them endemic,make the island a paradise for nature lovers. the animal world is also convincing,althrough it is not possible to affect large animals except for a few species.for this,the bird world is represented even more arsenically

the island of hisponiola is divided into two countries dominican republic and haiti.twobythird the island belongs to the territory of the dominican republic and onebythird to haiti.althrough both countries are on island,the landscape,cultural and social diffrences are huge.

the varied climate,geography as well as plants and fauna found in the country should not only attract you to visite,but invite you to explore.......

history

the island of hisponiola(la isla espanola) was the first colony in the new world settled by spain.

as such,it served as the logistical vase of the conseqest of most of the western hemisphere.christopher first sighted the island in 1492 towards the end of his voyage to "the indies".

for two countries the spanish ruled the island untill western hisponiola (present-day haiti) came under french control in 1697.the east still remained under spanish control.this created a divison that still exists today,with the dominican republic and haiti sharing the island.

finaly, and after decades of unstable political situation,on 27 february 1844,the territory of santo domingo recovered its sovereighity and declared its independence as the dominican republic.
